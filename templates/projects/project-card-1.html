By Matej Jan

I love spending time outside with my sketchbook! However, I rarely take the time to do it. There is this false notion that you need to take a lot of time and dive into details...

To counter this I decided to develop a technique where I spend at most 5-10 minutes drawing, just enough for a short break, but the results still look spectacular. To share it with you, I made a micro-course called Sketch5 where you learn how to quickly and efficiently draw the environment around you while you enjoy being outside in nature.

It only takes 5 minutes a day and you will be done in 5 days. matejjan.com/sketch5
