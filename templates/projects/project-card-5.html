#naturefoodeveryday

Spend 5 seconds to feel the connection with raw food — By Phoebe Fu

I’m doing a #naturefoodeveryday challenge where we take a moment to appreciate nature for providing us with amazing food. To join me in this challenge, take a picture of the raw materials before you cook your meal, spend a few seconds to feel the connection with what nature gives us, and pass it on to your food enthusiast friends.

Take a picture of the raw materials before you cook your meal, spend a few seconds to feel the connection with what nature gives us. Post the picture on FB or Instagram and pass it on by tagging your food enthusiast friends.
